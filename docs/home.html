The Artist

Erick Alves has been an illustrator and book designer for over five years. Keeping some focus on fantasy, sci-fi and horror, he also goes into children's books. In addition to working with publishers such as Skript, Pendragon, Vitrola, Sem Tinta, Skull, Flyve, Círculos Soturnos and others, he also works with independent authors.
